Dashboard for World Development Indicators

The most current and accurate global development data of our world is provided online by The World Bank. Unfortunately, one quickly becomes lost after downloading this dataset as there are over 10000 development indicators available for each country. To obtain a better overview of this dataset I programmed a dashboard that lets you explore the different indicators using a choropleth map. Additionally, the development of two countries over time can be compared directly in a scatter plot.
